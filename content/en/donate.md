## Support Our Community

KJMC Uzhhorod exists thanks to the generosity of people who believe in our mission. Your donation helps us:

- **Humanitarian aid (chesed)** — helping war-affected families, elderly, and those in need
- **Premises** — our current space has extreme temperature issues (-12°C in winter, +35°C in summer)
- **Ministry and outreach** — Scripture School, Mukachevo mission, youth programs
- **Equipment** — sound, streaming, and furnishing for our congregation
